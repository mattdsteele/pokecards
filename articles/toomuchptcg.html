How to tell if you play too much P: TCG


Here are a few humerous ways to tell if you play too much P: TCG. Remeber, there can be too much of a good thing.
  • You framed the first booster pack you ever got.
  • You bought a plane ticket to Japan just to get that promo Pikachu card.
  • You quote passages from the rulebook as if it were the Bible.
  • You've spent over $300,000 worth of Jungle boosters.
  • You've robbed an ATM to get money to buy Blackout.
  • You've ever beaten up a little kid to get that Oddish you need.
  • You're the reason the Pojo gets over 50,000 hits per day.
  • You have over 17 binders full of Jungle foils.
  • You've bookmarked PokéCards as your starting home page.
  • You built a shrine for your Charizard.
  • You skipped school for three days to be the first in line for Fossil.
  • Your Scyther has been played so much that the holographic background wore off. (I've seen this before!)
  • Your wallpaper is made up of Pikachu cards.
  • When you lose at P: TCG you call upon Gyarados to seek revenge.
  • You enjoy watching that P: TCG commercial. (And bought one of this "wrist cams").
  • You saw errors in that P: TCG commercial. (I've found three.)
  • You paid Wizards $3.5 million to build a store next to your house.
  • You see no problem in paying $13 for a booster pack.
    And the #1 way of telling you play too much P: TCG...
  • You've actually paid $105 for a Charizard.
