Cards to look out for in Fossil


Well, now that fossil is out, I bet you are all drooling over the brand new cards...So I decided to list some of the cards that are (in my opinion) the best:
  • Geodude: This card has MAJOR damage potential (with luck). Fighting Type, Basic Pokémon, 50 HP, Weakness Grass, one retreat...seems pretty average. But here's his awesome attack: For one Fighting and one colorless energy you flip a coin until it lands on tails. The amount of heads x 10 is the damage. Get lucky, and this is one mean attack.
  • Aerodactyl: Pokémon can't evolve? Need I say more?
  • Magmar: Just a good basic, mean to use early in the game.
  • Muk: Pokémon can't use their Pokémon Powers? Can we say anti-aerodactyl?
  • Mew: De-evolution beam returns the topmost evolution to your opponents hand? For example, if a Gyrados had 30 damage, it would be turned back into a Magikarp, and be K.O.'d!
  • Kabuto: Let's you see your opponent's hand...wow!

These are just a few of the great cards in fossil...They could be different in the American set, so watch out!
