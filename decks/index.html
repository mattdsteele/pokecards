Decks
Here you will find information on decks that I have made, other people have sent in, or decks from Nintendo or Scrye Magazine. Feel free to print them off and use as templates for your own decks.
If you have a deck that you think is good, send it in. Send the information in like this: (4X) Charmander. If you have any comments on the deck, such as a strategy useful, write that down under your cards. I can't promise that every deck will get in, but I can say that I won't reject any because of "not being good". Also, when you send the decks in, be sure to put your name & e-mail at the bottom. If I don't have this info, I'll send a message back asking for it. Thanks.
