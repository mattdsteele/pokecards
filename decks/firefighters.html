Trainer Comments/Strategy:
The Pokémon Fire Fighters (pretty original name, huh?), my 2nd deck, was made over the course of...5 minutes. I made it up the day before a tournament because none of my other decks were complete. There is a lack of trainers, but the ones I have are there for a purpose. The goal of this deck is to get out the Machamp (or if your're playing Psychic, Charizard) and start to beat down. However, this is a pretty slow deck, so just make sure you don't play Haymakers. I'm pretty much undefeated (Prize-wise) with this deck, but then again, I haven't played any haymakers.
