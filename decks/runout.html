Runout

Pokémon
4 Mewtwo

Trainer
4 Imposter Professor Oak
4 Bill

Energy
48 Psychic

Trainer Comments/Strategy:
You're probably thinking, "What is wrong with this kid?". This is a stall deck. The object is to win by making the opponent run out of cards (hence the Imposter Professor Oaks). You accomplish this by using Mewtwo's move barrier. You get Energy and discard it, using the move. There are a few flaws, though, such as running out of cards while trying to find a Mewtwo.
