Trainer Comments/Strategy:
This deck is an Energy Removal/Beatdown with the added bonus of never running out of Energy (with the Retrievals). There are no search cards, because it's pretty easy to make do with whatever you get at the start until you get what you really want (i.e. Venusaur, Nidoqueen and Pinsir). Also, you can use Venusaur's Pokémon Power to move energy off of a dying Pokémon onto another (see where I got the name of the deck now?). I would like to replace the Tangelas with Scythers, but unfortunately, I haven't been able to get any Scythers.
